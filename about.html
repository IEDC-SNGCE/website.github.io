Welcome to Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering (SNGCE). Today, you will find that we are better positioned than ever to address the grand challenges of the coming century. At SNGCE, we are putting knowledge to practice with innovations in critical areas like Communications, Design engineering, Embedded systems, Naval architecture, and Cybersecurity, just to name a few. We are a place where innovation is expected and our work today will impact our future lives in ways we cannot yet imagine. The college champions research and education that is multi-disciplinary in nature. We encourage multidisciplinary collaborations among all our professional colleges, other institutions, industry and government to ensure the institute is poised to succeed in our rapidly changing world.
